Biography

I sucessfully defended my phd thesis on June 15, 2021! I am a Ph.D. now!

I obtained the Ph.D. degree from the College of Biomedical Engineering & Instrument Science, Zhejiang University. Currently, I am a research assistant at IDEA lab, UNC-CH, advised by Dr. Gang Li. I mainly work on surface mesh-based deep learning methods for analyzing cortical surface data. Previously, I received the B.Eng degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2016.
