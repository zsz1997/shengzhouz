Biography

I am currently a 3rd-year PhD Student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), China, under the supervision of Prof. Yong Xia. I am also a visiting student at the School of Computer Science, University of Adelaide (UoA), Australia, under the supervision of Prof. Chunhua Shen.

My research interests mainly focus on deep learning technologies for medical image analysis, especially annotation-limited 3D medical image segmentation.

I am looking for a post-doc position or a medical-AI related job!
